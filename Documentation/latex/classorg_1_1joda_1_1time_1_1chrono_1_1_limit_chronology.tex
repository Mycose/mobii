\subsection{Detailed Description}
Wraps another \hyperlink{classorg_1_1joda_1_1time_1_1_chronology}{Chronology} to impose limits on the range of instants that the fields within a \hyperlink{classorg_1_1joda_1_1time_1_1_chronology}{Chronology} may support. The limits are applied to both Date\-Time\-Fields and Duration\-Fields. 

Methods in \hyperlink{classorg_1_1joda_1_1time_1_1_date_time_field}{Date\-Time\-Field} and \hyperlink{classorg_1_1joda_1_1time_1_1_duration_field}{Duration\-Field} throw an Illegal\-Argument\-Exception whenever given an input instant that is outside the limits or when an attempt is made to move an instant outside the limits. 

\hyperlink{classorg_1_1joda_1_1time_1_1chrono_1_1_limit_chronology}{Limit\-Chronology} is thread-\/safe and immutable.
