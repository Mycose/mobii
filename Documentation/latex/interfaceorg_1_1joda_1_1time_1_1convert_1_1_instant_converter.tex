\subsection{Detailed Description}
\hyperlink{interfaceorg_1_1joda_1_1time_1_1convert_1_1_instant_converter}{Instant\-Converter} defines how an object is converted to milliseconds/chronology. 

The two methods in this interface must be called in order, as the {\ttfamily get\-Instant\-Millis} method relies on the result of the {\ttfamily get\-Chronology} method being passed in.
