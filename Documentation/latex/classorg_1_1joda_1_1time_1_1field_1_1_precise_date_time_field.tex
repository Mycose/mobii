\subsection{Detailed Description}
Precise datetime field, composed of two precise duration fields. 

This \hyperlink{classorg_1_1joda_1_1time_1_1_date_time_field}{Date\-Time\-Field} is useful for defining Date\-Time\-Fields that are composed of precise durations, like time of day fields. If either duration field is imprecise, then an \hyperlink{classorg_1_1joda_1_1time_1_1field_1_1_imprecise_date_time_field}{Imprecise\-Date\-Time\-Field} may be used instead. 

\hyperlink{classorg_1_1joda_1_1time_1_1field_1_1_precise_date_time_field}{Precise\-Date\-Time\-Field} is thread-\/safe and immutable.
