\subsection{Detailed Description}
An immutable duration specifying a length of time in milliseconds. 

A duration is defined by a fixed number of milliseconds. There is no concept of fields, such as days or seconds, as these fields can vary in length. A duration may be converted to a \hyperlink{classorg_1_1joda_1_1time_1_1_period}{Period} to obtain field values. This conversion will typically cause a loss of precision however. 

\hyperlink{classorg_1_1joda_1_1time_1_1_duration}{Duration} is thread-\/safe and immutable.
