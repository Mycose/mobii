Round to the highest whole unit of this field. The value of this field and all fields of a higher magnitude may be incremented in order to achieve this result. The fractional millis that cannot be expressed in whole increments of this field are set to minimum. 

For example, a datetime of 2002-\/11-\/02\-T23\-:34\-:56.\-789, rounded to the highest whole hour is 2002-\/11-\/03\-T00\-:00\-:00.\-000. 

The default implementation calls round\-Floor, and if the instant is modified as a result, adds one field unit. Subclasses are encouraged to provide a more efficient implementation.
